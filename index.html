Frequently Asked Questions

How does soil analysis work?

We analyze the uploaded soil image using AI and combine it with real-time weather and location data from Open-Meteo to recommend the best crops.

Is location mandatory?

Yes. Location helps us fetch accurate climate data (temperature, humidity, rainfall) and understand regional soil patterns.

Are recommendations guaranteed?

No. This is a decision-support tool using AI models. Always verify results with local agricultural experts.

What weather data is used?

We fetch real-time temperature and humidity, plus the total rainfall from the last 365 days using the Open-Meteo API.

Can I switch between Acres and Hectares?

Yes! Use the unit toggle below the land size input. The value auto-converts when you switch, and all reports reflect your chosen unit.

How does the chatbot work?

After analysis, a chat icon appears at the bottom-right. Click it to ask follow-up questions about your crop recommendations. You can expand it to fullscreen.

Can I download my report?

Yes! After analysis, scroll to the bottom of the report section and click "Download Report as PDF". It saves a complete PDF file to your device.

Is Hindi language supported?

Yes. Use the language toggle button in the top navigation bar to switch between English and Hindi for all interface text.

How do I start a new analysis?

After an analysis is complete, a "Start New Analysis" button appears. Click it to reset everything and start fresh â€” no page refresh needed.

What crops does the system support?

The AI can recommend over 20 crops including rice, maize, cotton, wheat, lentils, various pulses, fruits (mango, banana, apple), and cash crops (coffee, jute, coconut).
